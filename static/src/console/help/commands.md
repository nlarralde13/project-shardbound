# Commands

## help

List available commands or show help for a command.

Usage: help [command]

Examples:
- help
- help look

## look

Look around or at a specific target.

Usage: look [target]

Examples:
- look
- look chest

## move

Move in a direction.

Usage: move <n|s|e|w>

Examples:
- move north
- go e

## inv

Show your inventory.

Usage: inv

Examples:
- inv

## use

Use an item in your inventory.

Usage: use <item>

Examples:
- use potion

## equip

Equip an item from your inventory.

Usage: equip <item>

Examples:
- equip sword
